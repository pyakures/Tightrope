What makes Tightrope Different?

Yes, there are a variety of scheduler applications that manage the day-to-day operations of people's lives. Tightrope is different - it places emphasis on mental wellness and prioritizes an optimized work:life balance.

To mantain a healthy work:life balance, individuals must be active in developing their mental wellness and understanding common stresses in their lives. Because of this, the developers of Tightrope have built the application on the foundation of three principles: Intentionality, Education, and Mindfulness.

By employing foundational principles, Tightrope aims to support indivuduals in unlocking their true potential by promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Intentionality

Understand the cause and effects of your decisions.

Education

Know how to support your mental wellness with tools.

Mindfulness

Be conscious and aware of your mental health.

Features

Event Classifications

Tightrope initially appears as a regular scheduler, equiped with adding, editing, and deleting events. Additionally, the events then can be categorized into leisure vs. work with an associated stress level for that event.

 Stress Survey

The application optimizes the user's schedule by presenting a stress survey. The user can use the stress survey to reflect on their schedule. The data collected is then used to optimize future schedules for the user.

 Stress Gauge and Summary

The application then is able to determine a summary of stress for the user for the current day, week, or month that is then displayed graphically as the stress gauge.
